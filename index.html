NLP and Society Lab @ UU

Hello! We're the NLP and Society lab, part of the Natural Language Processing group at the Computer Science department at Utrecht University (NL). We're interested in:

  • Analyzing and modeling the relation between language and society using computational methods (computational sociolinguistics, Nguyen et al. Computational Linguistics 2016).
  • The development of NLP tools to learn more about societal phenomena (NLP and computational social science).
  • And, as NLP is increasingly used in society we focus on developing robust and interpretable NLP models.
